  • What benefit comes from breaking down the construction of bigger projects into smaller pieces? The breaking down of large projects into smaller pieces allows us to troubleshoot each individual piece and check every individual piece before moving on,so that when we face an issue we don't have to look through a massive amount of code.
  • Why did we constantly check and test our code? What could go wrong if we did not test our code thoroughly? If we didn't check our code thoroughly and constantly check the smaller parts, we wouldn't know where the problem was if we only checked it when everything was done.
