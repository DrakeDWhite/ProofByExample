What is Proof By Example?

Proof by Example is a website created to help students around the world. The main purpose of the website is to teach students of all levels intermediate to advanced mathematical concepts, logic, and anything else that James Marshall Reber may find important. He is more than welcome to edit this blurb as he likes.
